Ecosystem

Everything you need
to deploy & earn

Four interconnected layers make NEXLUM a complete infrastructure platform for on-chain AI.

Agent Runtime
Infrastructure Layer
▼
Blazing-fast execution environment for autonomous AI agents. Deploy in seconds. Scale to millions of operations without touching a server. Built on a decentralized node network with guaranteed uptime SLAs enforced by on-chain staking collateral.
NexBridge
Cross-Chain Protocol
▼
Seamlessly bridge agent logic and $LUM liquidity across any EVM-compatible chain. Your agents operate everywhere — Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, and beyond — without redeployment. One configuration, infinite reach.
LumLens Analytics
Data & Insights
▼
Real-time on-chain analytics for every agent deployed on NEXLUM. Track performance, earnings, task completion rates, and uptime from one unified dashboard. Full transparency — every metric is pulled directly from chain.
NexDAO
Governance
▼
The decentralized governing body of the NEXLUM protocol. Propose, discuss, vote, and steer the ecosystem with your $LUM stake. Every protocol parameter — from fee rates to treasury allocations — is governed entirely by the community.
How It Works

From wallet to
working AI agent

Three steps. No code. No servers. No gatekeepers.

1
Connect your wallet
Sign in with any EVM-compatible wallet. No KYC, no account creation. Your wallet is your identity on NEXLUM. Hold $LUM to unlock the full platform.
2
Configure your AI agent
Use the NEXLUM agent builder to define your agent's objective, data sources, trigger conditions, and output actions — all in plain language. Deploy it on-chain in one transaction.
3
Earn while your agent works
List your agent on the NEXLUM Marketplace. Other users pay in $LUM to use it. You earn a share of every interaction, automatically settled on-chain, 24/7.
